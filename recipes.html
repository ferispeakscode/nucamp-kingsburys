Easy Apple Cake

3 cups
Flour
2 cups
Sugar
1 cup
Oil
3
Eggs
1 tsp.
Salt
1 tsp.
Baking Soda
2 tsp.
Vanilla
3 cups
Apples
1 cup
Walnuts

Directions

  1. Mix all of the ingredients together in a large container.
  2. Pour mixture into a greased sheet cake pan and bake at 325° for 1 hour.

Easy Peach Cobbler

Ingredients

4 cups
Sliced Kingsbury's Orchard peaches (about 6 peaches)
2 cup
Sugar, divided
1/2 cup
Butter or Margarine
3/4 cup
All-Purpose Flour
3/4 cup
Milk
2 tsp.
Baking Powder
(as desired)
Vanilla Ice Cream

Directions

  1. Combine peaches at 1/2 cup sugar. Let stand 15 minutes or until a syrup forms.
  2. Melt better in an 11 x 7 inch baking dish.
  3. Stir together remaining 1/2 cup sugar, flour, baking powder, and milk. Pour mixture over melted butter (do not stir).
  4. Spoon peaches over mixture (do not stir).
  5. Bake at 350° for 55 minutes.
  6. Serve warm cobbler with vanilla ice cream.
